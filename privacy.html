Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Elevia Care values your privacy. This policy describes how we collect and protect your data when using our health and wellness app for seniors.

1. Information We Collect

  • Registration Data: Name, email, and age (to ensure the user is part of our target audience).
  • Health and Routine Data: Information voluntarily entered by you, such as medication reminders, supplements, habit tracking, and progress in daily challenges.
  • Payment Information: Processed exclusively by the Google Play Store. We do not store your credit card data on our servers.

2. How We Use Information

Your data is used to:

  • Personalize your daily challenges and health reminders.
  • Ensure access to educational PDF content.
  • Manage your subscription and the 7-day free trial period.
  • Improve application features and performance.

3. Data Security

We implement technical security measures to protect your health data and personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes. Data may only be shared with essential service providers (such as server hosting) or as required by law.

5. Your Rights and Account Deletion

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time. To delete your account and all associated data, you can do so directly within the app settings or by contacting us via the email below.

6. Subscriptions and Trial Period

The app offers 7 days of free use. After this period, the monthly or annual subscription will be charged via Google Play, as selected during the onboarding process.
